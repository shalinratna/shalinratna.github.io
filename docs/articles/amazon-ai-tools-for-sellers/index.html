ChatGPT: The AI Tool for Product Research

ChatGPT is a popular AI tool that uses natural language processing to analyze product reviews, ratings, and sales data. By analyzing this data, ChatGPT can identify top-selling products on Amazon and provide you with detailed insights into their performance.

To use ChatGPT, simply follow these steps:

  1. Open ChatGPT and enter your Amazon seller account credentials.
  2. Select the "Product Research" tab.
  3. Enter a keyword or product category to start searching for top-selling products.
  4. Review the results and select the products you want to analyze further.

By using ChatGPT, you can find top-selling products on Amazon in just minutes, saving you time and effort compared to manual research methods.
