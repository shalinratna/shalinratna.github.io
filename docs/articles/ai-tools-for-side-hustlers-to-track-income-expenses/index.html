Common Mistakes to Avoid When Managing Your Side Hustle's Finances

• Not separating personal and business expenses. • Ignoring tax deductions and credits. • Failing to review financial statements regularly.

• Set up a separate business bank account and track expenses accurately. • Utilize tax deductions and credits available for your side hustle. • Regularly review financial statements to identify areas for improvement.
