## Mint
Mint is one of the most popular personal finance apps that uses AI to track expenses automatically. With over 20 million users, Mint has become a go-to solution for managing finances. Its AI-powered tool, Personalized Spending Alerts, provides real-time notifications when you're about to overspend or exceed your budget.
