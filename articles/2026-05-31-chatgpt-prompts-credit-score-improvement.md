## Common Mistakes to Avoid

* **Don't neglect your credit reports**: Ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date.
* **Avoid new credit inquiries**: Limit new applications to minimize the impact on your score.
* **Pay bills on time**: Timely payments are crucial for maintaining a healthy credit score.
* **Monitor your credit utilization**: Keep debt levels below 30% of total credit limits.
