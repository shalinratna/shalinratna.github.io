## Common Mistakes Solo Entrepreneurs Make with AI

* Over-reliance on AI for creative tasks (leaving room for human innovation)
* Insufficient training or setup for AI-powered tools
* Failure to regularly review and refine AI outputs

• Use AI to augment, not replace, your own skills and creativity.
• Invest time in setting up and training your AI tools correctly.
• Regularly review and refine AI outputs to ensure accuracy and relevance.
