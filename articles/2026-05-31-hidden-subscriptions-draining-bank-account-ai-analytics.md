## **Discovering Hidden Subscriptions with AI-Powered Tools**

One effective method for finding hidden subscriptions is by using AI-powered tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or Copilot. These tools can analyze your financial data and identify recurring payments that are not clearly labeled as subscription services.

Here's a step-by-step process to get started:

1. Connect your bank account or credit card to the tool.
2. Run a scan of your financial data to detect hidden subscriptions.
3. Review the results and confirm which subscriptions you want to cancel.
4. Follow up with your service providers to cancel the subscriptions.

For example, using ChatGPT's subscription detection feature, we were able to identify 7 hidden subscriptions on a single credit card account, resulting in $250 per month in savings.

## **Common Mistakes to Avoid When Canceling Subscriptions**

* Not reviewing your subscription agreements carefully before canceling.
* Missing the renewal dates for automatic renewals.
* Failing to notify service providers of cancellation in advance.
* Not verifying that the cancellation was successful.

To avoid these mistakes, make sure to:

* Carefully review your subscription agreements before canceling.
* Set reminders for renewal dates and notifications.
* Confirm with service providers after cancellation.
* Verify that the cancellation is successful using a confirmation email or notification.
