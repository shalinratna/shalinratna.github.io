## **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

When using AI-powered estate planning tools, there are a few common mistakes to watch out for:

* Not updating your will after major life changes (e.g., marriage, divorce, or inheritance).
* Failing to include specific beneficiaries or assets in your plan.
* Not reviewing and revising your plan regularly.

To avoid these mistakes, make sure to:

* Regularly review and update your plan with the AI tool.
* Double-check your input for accuracy and completeness.
* Take advantage of online resources and support from the estate planning service provider.
