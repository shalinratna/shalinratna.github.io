Mint: A Popular AI Budgeting Tool

Mint is one of the most widely used AI budgeting apps, offering a comprehensive financial management platform. Open the app and link your accounts to track your spending, create a budget, and set financial goals. Step 1: Link all your financial accounts to Mint to get a complete picture of your finances.

YNAB (You Need a Budget): A Budgeting Method with AI Integration

YNAB offers a unique budgeting method that combines human intuition with AI-driven insights. This app helps you manage your finances by assigning jobs to every dollar, ensuring you're making the most of your money.
